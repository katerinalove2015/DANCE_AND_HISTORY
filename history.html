The History of Dance

Dance has been part of human culture for thousands of years. People danced to celebrate, tell stories, and express emotions.

Ancient Times

The earliest dances were performed in ancient civilizations such as Egypt, Greece, and India. Dance was often connected to religion and festivals.

Middle Ages

During the Middle Ages, people enjoyed folk dances at celebrations and special events. Many dances were performed in groups.

Ballet Begins

Ballet started in Italy during the Renaissance and later became popular in France. It is now one of the most famous dance styles in the world.

Modern Dance

In the 20th century, new styles such as jazz, hip hop, and contemporary dance became popular around the world.
